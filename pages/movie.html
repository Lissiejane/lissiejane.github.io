About Fire Walk With Me

Fire Walk With Me is David Lynch's 1992 prequel movie to his cult following TV show, Twin Peaks. Fire Walk With Me shows the audience who and what Laura Palmer encounters during the last week of her life. Witness chilling encounters previously unknown to the viewer and see for yourself WHAT Laura saw, WHO was there, and WHERE is it the unfortunate few find themselves? Find out in Fire Walk With Me.
