About Twin Peaks (1990-1991)

The original two seasons of Twin Peaks revolved around the murder of a local teen, Laura Palmer. The circumstances around her death are mysterious- is it apart of something bigger? Logs, pies, giants, and one-armed men await! Do you need new shoes? Are the owls what they seem in Twin Peaks? Will Cooper ever find that damn fine cup of coffee? Find out in David Lynch's Twin Peaks.
