The owls are not what they seem in Twin Peaks...

Join local law enforcement, Harry S. Truman, and Special Agent Dale Cooper in their mission to find who or what killed Laura Palmer. Laura was a seemingly well-adjusted, well-liked and generally revered member of the small, rural logging community of Twin Peaks, WA. Locals will tell you, "There's a sort of evil out there... Something very, very strange in these old woods.". Do you know who killed Laura Palmer?
